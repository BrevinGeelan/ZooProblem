Zoo Animals

Bears

  • Ollie
  • Mona

Extremely strong mammals, multiple species found throughout the world. Ollie and Mona are Kodiak Grizzly Bears. They may look fluffy, but anyone interacting with them should use extreme caution..

https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bear

Giraffes

  • Frankie
  • Coconut

Giraffes like Frankie and Coconut are long necked grazing mammals. Every once in a while, you may find them swinging their necks at each other as a way to assert dominance over one another.

https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Giraffe

Lions

  • Mella
  • Karl

Male lions like Karl are considered to be King of the African Savannas.. But adult females like Mella are nothing to shake a stick at!

https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lion

Monkeys

  • Cookie
  • Earl
  • Banana Pudding

Chimpanzees like Cookie, Earl and Banana Pudding are the closest relative to humans, they are very smart, have been observed using tools, and some chimpanzee communities have even been observed waging war against each other!

https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Monkey

Alligators

  • Wren
  • Aspen
  • Mika

Extremely large reptiles that are believed to be living dinosaurs. The largest American Alligator ever recorded was 17 feet, 5 inches long! Wren, Aspen and Mika may not be quite that large, but Aspen, our male, is 12 feet long and weighs over 650 pounds!

https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Alligator
